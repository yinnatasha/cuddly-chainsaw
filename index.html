 My Journey

 I’ve always believed that every step in life shapes who we are, and my educational journey has been a big part of that. I started my learning adventure at SK Pasir Putih, my primary school, where I first developed a love for knowledge and the world around me. Those early years taught me the importance of curiosity and hard work. After primary school, I continued my education at MRSM Tun Mustapha  (MRSM TMT), a boarding school that played a huge role in shaping my personal growth. Living away from home and being part of such a close-knit community helped me build independence, discipline, and strong friendships. It was there that I was introduced to the world of technology, and I became fascinated by the endless possibilities it offered. 

After completing my SPM, I decided to pursue a Foundation in IT, which marked the next big step in my academic journey. It gave me a deeper understanding of the tech world and helped me discover my passion for multimedia. Fast forward to today, and I’m now pursuing a degree in Multimedia Technology at University Malaysia Sabah Labuan International Campus. Each stage of my education has been a stepping stone that has brought me closer to my goals, and I’m excited to continue this journey of learning and growth.
